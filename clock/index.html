Link options

Tiny embed — minimal UI, fits small iframes:

  • ?tiny=1 or ?mode=tiny
  • ?seconds=0 — hide seconds (HH:MM)
  • ?bg=%23000&color=%2300ff00 — background + time color (hex encoded)

Full page — default; analog + digital:

  • ?analog=0 — hide analog face
  • ?digital=0 — hide digital block
  • ?tz=America%2FNew_York — IANA timezone (optional)
  • ?12 or ?format=12 — 12-hour digital
  • ?bg=…&fg=…&accent=… — CSS colors (hex or names)
