UUID generator

Generate random UUIDs (v4). Choose how many, then copy. Uses crypto.randomUUID() when available, fallback to crypto.getRandomValues().

About

UUID v4 is 122 bits of randomness in the form xxxxxxxx-xxxx-4xxx-yxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx (hex). Safe for IDs, tokens, and nonces.

  • Count: 1–100 per click.
  • One UUID per line in the output.
